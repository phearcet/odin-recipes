Description

This is a recipe on how to make a delicious Chili dog. It takes little time and skill but yields increidible results.

Ingredients

  • Hot dog
  • Bread
  • Mustard and Ketchup

Steps

  1. Boil hot dog until its cooked
  2. Slice open the bread, you may toast it for better texture
  3. Put hot dog in the bread and put the sauce on the meat. Done!
