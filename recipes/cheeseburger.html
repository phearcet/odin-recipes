Cheese Burger

Description

This is a recipe on how to make a delicious cheese burger. It takes little time and skill but yields increidible results.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Beef Patty
  • American Cheese Slice

Steps

  1. Cook Beef Patty on a cast iron pan.
  2. Use the residule heat and oil to toast the bun.
  3. layer it on top of each other, done!
